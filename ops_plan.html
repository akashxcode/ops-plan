Operations Plan Overview

Akashdeep Singh

Reason for Plan

Fiscal year internal help desk operations plan.

Mission

Enhance support, resolve issues, and improve experience.

Vision

Future-ready help desk, efficient, proactive, and user-focused.

SMART Goals

  • Reduce response time by 10% by year-end.
  • Increase first contact resolution rate by 10% by year-end.
  • Decrease incident resolution time by 10% by year-end.
  • Increase self-service effectiveness by 10% by year-end.
  • Decrease backlog by 15% by year-end.
  • Decrease incidents/requests by 5% by year-end.

User Benefits

  • Faster solutions improve productivity.
  • Fewer escalations save time.
  • Happier users lead to smoother operations.
  • Better self-service reduces reliance on support.
  • Less backlog improves efficiency.
  • Fewer incidents create smoother workflow.
